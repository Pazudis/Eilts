Past Simple teikumu veidošana

Lai angļu valodā izveidotu teikumu pagātnes laikā (past simple), jālieto darbības vārda pagātnes formu. Pagātnes laika teikuma vispārīgā struktūra:

Apgalvojuma teikumi:

I played We played
You played You played
He / she / it played They played

Jautājumu teikumi:

Did I play? Did we play?
Did you play? Did you play?
Did he / she / it play? Did they play?

Negatīvi teikumi:

I did not play We did not play
You did not play You did not play
He / she / it did not play They did not play

Piemēri

1. She visited London last summer. (Pagājušajā vasarā viņa apmeklēja Londonu.)
2. I completed my homework yesterday. (Es vakar izpildīju mājasdarbu.)
3. They went to the beach on Saturday. (Sestdien viņi devās uz pludmali.)
